The Afterlife had Wood Paneling

What happens after you die? Simple - your implanted soulkeeper uploads you to a virtual world designed to simulate your brain so you can continue living. (After)life in New Detroit is certainly no paradise, but it beats the alternative, and you get to live forever.

Except now, someone's died, and unless you can find out who and how, anyone could be next.

The Afterlife had Wood Paneling is an interactive hypertext novel written with twine v1.3.6. It is open source.
